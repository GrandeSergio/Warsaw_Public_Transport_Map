Warsaw Public Transport Map

Welcome to the Public Transportation Map application! This interactive map provides information about public transportation networks in Warsaw, including buses, railways, trams, and the metro.

Our mission is to make public transportation data accessible and user-friendly. With this map, you can explore various public transportation routes, stops, and stations in Warsaw and plan your journeys more efficiently.

Features

  • View bus stops, railway stations, tram stops, and metro stations on the map.
  • Toggle different transportation layers for better visualization.
  • Click on markers to view detailed information about specific stops and stations.
  • Explore the map seamlessly with zoom and pan functionality.

Transportation

Bus Stop     Bus stops are designated locations where buses pick up and drop off passengers. They form the backbone of public transportation systems.
Railway Station     Railway stations are key transportation hubs for trains. They connect various cities and regions through rail networks.
Tram Station     Tram stations are stops along tramway tracks where trams pick up and drop off passengers. They are commonly seen in urban public transportation networks.
Subway Station     Subway stations, also known as metro stations, are underground railway stations. They are commonly found in urban areas and facilitate rapid transit.
